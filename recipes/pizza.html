Chicken Parmesan Pizza

Description

Here is a recipe for making an incredibly scrumptious chicken parmesan pizza.

This simply requires pre-bought frozen chicken nuggets - no faffing with having to make breaded chicken from scratch!

Ingredients

  • 12 frozen chicken nuggets
  • 1 x 12-inch pre-baked pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • Button mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup shredded parmesan (or equivalent)
  • 6 basil leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220 degrees celcius.
  2. Arrange nuggets in a single layer on a pizza pan.
  3. Bake nuggets in the oven for 10 minutes and then cut each nugget in half.
  4. Increase the oven temperate to 230 degrees celcius.
  5. Place pizza crust on pan, spread on marinara evenly, arrange nuggets and sliced mushrooms evenly, and sprinkle with mozzarella.
  6. Bake pizza in oven until heated through (around 5 minutes.)
  7. Sprinkle over parmesan and basil and bake for a further 3 minutes.
  8. Success!
