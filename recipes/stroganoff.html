Simple Beef Stroganoff

Description

This is a recipe for a very simple beef stroganoff that anyone can make!

Ingredients

  • 1 can of cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 package of dry onion soup mix
  • 1 pound of cubed beef stew meat
  • 4 ounces of cream cheese
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Whisk cream of mushroom soup, water and dry onion soup mix in slow cooker.
  2. Stir meat into soup mixture.
  3. Cover and cook on low until beef is tender (6 to 8 hours.)
  4. Stir cream cheese into beef until smooth.
  5. Cook pasta and serve with stroganoff.
